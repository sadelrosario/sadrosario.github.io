Off-Beat! Game Concept Art

Off-Beat! is a fighting game concept inspired by musical rhythms as its main mechanic. This was a group project for our Introduction to Game Development class. I provided major contributions for conceptualizing the game as well as creating the mock title screen and the concept art for the fighting interface for our game presentation.

Pubmat Illustration and Reusable Assets

These are some of the works I illustrated for the Blue Symphony Orchestra for their recruitment week with a given theme of circus animals. I was the only artist on board, incharged with creating the main poster and resuable assets. These were all done in less than two weeks along with other workloads.

Children's Book Illustration

These are illustrations made for my friend's writing. It follows the story of a lonely prince who befriends a cheerful selkie. My friend wanted to go for the mood as The Little Prince, these illustrations were heavily inspired by the emotional tones of that story.
